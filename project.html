Telehealth MVP Execution

Leading engineering execution and product roadmap for a new telemedicine platform.

Role

Project Manager Intern

Duration

June - Aug 2025

Focus

Product Strategy, Agile Delivery

The Challenge

Altheros Capital required a Minimum Viable Product (MVP) for a new Telehealth initiative. The challenge was to coordinate across backend, frontend, and design teams to deliver a functional platform within a tight summer deadline. There was a critical need to align 10+ stakeholders on features and scope.

The Solution

I took ownership of the engineering execution, implementing structured Agile processes to streamline development.

  • Translated high-level clinical operational requirements into a technical roadmap of 15+ features, including symptom intake and provider scheduling.
  • Established a Risk Register and effectively communicated status through weekly KPIs and velocity tracking.
  • Facilitated seamless collaboration between design and engineering teams using JIRA for sprint planning.

Outcome

The structured approach ensured the successful delivery of the MVP scope on time. The process improvements I introduced helped align the team of engineers and stakeholders, reducing ambiguity and accelerating decision-making.
